Familia Omnia




ABOUT US

We are a community which cares about family, religious values and tradition. We strive to be an active community creating content for traditional white families. Familia Omnia wants to create educational content for children and their families. This includes: educational videos, books, podcasts and articles, all made by our team. We are searching for contributors who share our views and want to help us achieve this plan.























OUR GOALS

Our goals are to create educational content for children of all ages and abilities, to help spread truth and promote tradition, culture and religious values. We hope to have content in the near future on the following:

- History (Teaching children and teens the real history of their people. For instance; one class could be educating children/teens on the impact that the Roman Empire has had on Europe, be that through technology, expansion, administration, or the engineering feats that they acomplished)
- Science (Educating children and teens on the world of science)
- Religion (Educating children and teens about the religions of the world with a particular focus on Christianity and bible studies)
- Early learning books (such as ABCs)
- Modern Foreign Languages (Teaching kids their native tounge and other useful languages to gear them up for the future)
- Family upbringing guides (We hope to have parents in our community being role models, sharing experiences and helping to teach young couples how to effectively raise their children)

There will be much more to come in the future, so please check back soon.

People are encouraged to help contribute in any way they can. You can do this through content creation, sharing your experiences, helping with language lessons and any other contributions you feel will make our vision a reality.
